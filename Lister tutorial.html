In depth Tutorial - Lister



Basics

Hi, welcome to Lister. Click on the heading to change the title. Press new list to duplicate the list. Finally, type in the bar to add an item.



Checking list items and adding Priorities

To check a list item, double click. To remove a checked symbol, left click. Finally, to toggle priorities, click once.



Adding completion dates

To add a completion date, fill in the mm/dd/yyyy section, your date will automatically come on your list item.



The rewards shop

To go to the rewards shop, click the tab on top of the input, redeem rewards for the amount of gold you have.


Analytics

Check analytics like the daily goal, how many tasks you have completed, and more in the Analytics section.



Motivation

Get motivated to complete tasks with famous quotes and take breaks with the break timer.


Thank you for using Lister!!!
